The Science Behind the Swing

Traditional golf balls use polybutadiene—a synthetic rubber that can take over 1,000 years to decompose. Greenscape changes the game.

We leverage cutting-edge material science to create a core from compressed starches and organic binders. The outer shell is a specialized, tough biopolymer designed to withstand 120+ mph swing speeds, yet activate its decomposition cycle when exposed to soil moisture and microbes.

It’s not just a ball; it’s an engineering marvel designed for the game you love and the planet you live on.
