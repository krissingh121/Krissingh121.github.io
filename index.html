The Environmental Impact

The hidden cost of traditional plastic golf balls.

300M+
An estimated 300 million golf balls are lost or discarded in the United States every year.
1,000
It takes between 100 and 1,000 years for traditional golf balls to decompose.
Toxic
During decomposition, balls release high quantities of heavy metals like dangerous levels of zinc into the surrounding flora and fauna.
Microplastics
Synthetic rubber and plastic polymer covers break down into microplastics that can be ingested by marine life.
