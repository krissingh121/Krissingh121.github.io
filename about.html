The Greenscape Story

Greenscape Golf began one day after questioning where the golf balls we hit into the woods and waterways go. They end up polluting our environment and courses.

Realizing that over 300 million golf balls are lost annually in the US alone, we knew we had a social responsibility to act. We combined environmental science with sports engineering to create a business where profit directly funds ecological restoration.
